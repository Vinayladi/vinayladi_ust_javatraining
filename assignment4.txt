1. Introduction

Microservices Architecture divides a large application into smaller independent services.

Each service:

Handles one business functionality
Has its own database
Can be deployed independently
Communicates using APIs
2. Proposed Microservices

The Online Food Delivery App can be divided into the following services:

User Service
Restaurant Service
Cart & Order Service
Payment Service
Delivery Service
Notification Service

Additional Components:

API Gateway
Service Discovery
